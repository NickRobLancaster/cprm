ABOUT US

Critical Period Risk Management (“CPRM”) is the Risk Management Division of Critical Period Insurance Agency Inc. (“CPIA”). For more than 45 years, our management team has been recognized worldwide by major OEMs, insurance companies and Fortune 500 companies for being innovators of custom risk management solutions. The byproduct of many of our engagements is the creation of new, first-of-a-kind, insurance policies and off-balance-sheet risk transfer solutions to address our client’s recurring risks and exposures. We are a quiet company, and we receive most of our engagements through client referrals. Due to the confidential nature of our engagements, we offer only general information regarding our practice online. The major emphasis of our practice today is in cybersecurity solutions wherein CPRM has formed a cybersecurity consortium with Lookout Inc. and AppGuard Inc. to help clients manage and control their cyber risk exposures with state-of-the-art, best-in-glass, cybersecurity software technology.
